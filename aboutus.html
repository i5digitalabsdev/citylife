About Us

CityLife is a real estate developer focused on creating quality signature residential developments
in Accra. Over the past fifteen (15) years since inception we have evolved from just building one-off
homes to developing high quality residentiaal homes that are safe and contemporary in preferred locations.




The Rapid increase in population has resulted in a large housing deficit in Ghana and at CityLife we are
poised to bridge the gap through innovative housing solutions. Working with experienced contractors,
architects and engineers we bring your dreaam into reality. We help residents enjoy a convenient
lifestyle within cities with state of the art amenities.

Vision & Mission

Vision - Become the top-of-mind real estate developer of choice within Ghana's cities.


Mission - To deliver quality, attractive and affordable luxury residential real estate projects in the country.

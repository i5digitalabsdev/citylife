HOW TO BUY

Find out which Payment Terms suits you.



Cash Buyers

  • $5,000 reservation fee
  • 21 days to sign and return Sale and Purchase Agreement (“SPA”) and pay 25% of the purchase price
  • 4 quarterly installments of 15% of the purchase price beginning 3 months after signing SPA
  • 15% of the purchase price minus reservation fee on completion of the apartment.

Mortgage Buyers

  • $5,000 reservation fee
  • 21 days to sign and return Sale and Purchase Agreement (“SPA”) and pay 25% of the purchase price
  • 2 quarterly installments of 12.5% of the purchase price
  • Mortgage disbursement on completion of the apartment.
